MIS Dashboards That Leadership Actually Uses

Structured reporting frameworks for Tally/ERP users across accounting and operations.

15%
Accuracy Improvement
Multi
Dept. Dashboards

The Problem

Management reviews relied on ad-hoc Excel files emailed between departments. Data was inconsistent, versions conflicted, and follow-up consumed hours each week. Recurring operational issues had no documented resolution path.

What I Built

  • Advanced Excel and Google Sheets MIS reports with automated data pulls
  • Management review trackers with conditional formatting and exception alerts
  • KPI dashboards aligned to each department's operational metrics
  • SOPs and access control routines for every recurring reporting process

Outcome

Record-management inaccuracies dropped by approximately 15%. Leadership shifted from chasing updates to reviewing live dashboards. The reporting framework became the standard for all departmental MIS across the organization.
